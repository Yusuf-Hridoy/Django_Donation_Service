What is Nisab? Calculate your Zakat amount...

Nisab is the minimum amount that a Muslim must have before being obliged to zakat. The Nisab was set by Prophet Muhammad (SAW) at a rate equivalent to: 87.48 grams of gold and 612.36 grams of silver. Zakat is payable at 2.5% of the wealth one possesses above the nisab.
